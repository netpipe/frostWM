Licensing Information for pySVG

pySVG uses a dual licensing model that offers you a choice of either an open source license or a commercial license.
The open source license permits use of pySVG in open source projects or in applications that are not commercially used (see definition below).
The commercial license permits closed-source distribution of an application based on (the original or a customized version) pySVG.

What does "commercially used" mean?
The term "commerially used" refers to 
a) any redistribution for which you demand money 
b) any usage of pySVG in a program for which you demand money
c) any usage of pySVG in a company
